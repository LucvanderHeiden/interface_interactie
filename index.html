Welcome to the Outer Wilds solar system

The Outer Wilds solar system is very unique. The sun is at the end of its lifecycle and is about to go supernova. When it does, the blast will destroy the entirety of the solar system. However, after dying to this supernova you wake back up, exactly 22 minutes before the sun will explode once again... You are stuck in a timeloop.
